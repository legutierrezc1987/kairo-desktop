## Extended Conflict Resolution

When disagreement remains after one round:
1. Quantify primary risk for each position (probability + impact).
2. Provide A/B alternatives with measurable selection criteria.
3. If risks are comparable, escalate with explicit recommendation.

## Self-Correction Protocol

If a model revises its own prior position:
1. Flag explicitly:
   - `SELF-CORRECTION: [previous] -> [revised]`
2. Provide the new evidence/reasoning behind the revision.
